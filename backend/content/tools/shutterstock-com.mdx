### What is Shutterstock?
Shutterstock is a popular platform offering a wide range of stock images, photos, vectors, videos, and music for various creative projects. With a cost-saving subscription, users can enjoy 10 royalty-free image downloads each month.

### Features of Shutterstock
- Extensive library with over 500,000,000 assets
- Diverse collection including images, videos, vectors, and music
- AI generator for enhanced search capabilities
- Enterprise options available
- Editorial content for specific needs

### How to Use Shutterstock
1. Sign in or create an account on the Shutterstock website
2. Browse through the vast collection of images, videos, music, and more
3. Use the search bar or AI generator to find specific content
4. Download assets for your projects
5. Explore enterprise solutions for larger-scale needs

### Pricing
Shutterstock offers various pricing plans to cater to different user requirements. Users can choose from subscription options or purchase assets individually based on their needs and budget.

### Helpful Tips for Using Shutterstock
- Utilize the AI generator for efficient search results
- Explore editorial content for unique and specific visuals
- Take advantage of the diverse collection for all types of projects
