### What is Sunoh.ai?
Sunoh.ai is an AI Medical Scribe that revolutionizes clinical documentation by converting natural conversations between healthcare providers and patients into detailed clinical notes. It offers a seamless and efficient experience for doctors and patients, enhancing the speed and accuracy of documentation.

### Features of Sunoh.ai
- ### Ambient Listening Technology: Sunoh utilizes AI-powered ambient listening technology to capture and transcribe patient-provider conversations accurately.
- ### Dialogue Flow Generation: It uses AI and voice recognition to create a transcript of the dialogue flow between providers and patients.
- ### Clinical Document Draft Creation: Sunoh categorizes and summarizes content into appropriate Progress Note sections for easy review and importation into EHR systems.
- ### Order Entry Assistance: The AI scribe captures labs, imaging, procedures, medication orders, and follow-up visit details efficiently.
- ### Review and Summary: Sunoh streamlines the documentation process by allowing providers to review, modify, and merge content with pre-configured defaults seamlessly.

### How Sunoh.ai Works
1. ### Listens to the conversation: Sunoh listens to patient-provider conversations for accurate documentation.
2. ### Generates a dialogue flow: Utilizes AI and voice recognition to create a transcript of the conversation.
3. ### Creates a clinical document draft: Categorizes and summarizes content into Progress Note sections.
4. ### Assists with order entry: Captures labs, imaging, procedures, medication orders, and follow-up details.
5. ### Provides a summary for review: Allows providers to review, modify, and merge content with pre-configured defaults easily.
