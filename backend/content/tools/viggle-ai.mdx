### What is Viggle?
Viggle is an innovative AI-powered platform that allows users to animate characters for free. It offers a unique and creative way to bring characters to life through controllable video generation.

### Features of Viggle
- ### Controllable Video Generation: Powered by JST-1, Viggle utilizes the first video-3D foundation model with actual physics understanding, enabling users to make characters move as they desire.
- ### Creative Community: Join a community of over 4 million members who are passionate about creating and animating characters using Viggle's AI technology.

### How does Viggle work?
Viggle offers various functionalities to bring your characters to life:
- ### Mix: Use the `/mix` feature to blend a character image into a motion video.
- ### Animate: Utilize the `/animate` tool to add motion to a static character with a text motion prompt.
- ### Move: With the `/move` function, you can control the motion of your image according to your preferences.
