### What is DeepAI?
DeepAI is a platform that offers a variety of artificially intelligent tools for naturally creative humans. It includes AI Chat, AI Image Generator, AI Video Generator, and AI Music Generator.

### Features of DeepAI
- AI Video Generator: Turn your still images and text prompts into brief video animations.
- AI Image Generator: Enter a prompt, pick an art style, and DeepAI will bring your idea to life.
- AI Image Editor: Edit images in seconds by entering a simple prompt.
- AI Characters: Dive deep into conversation with characters like sports personalities, celebrities, TV show characters, game characters, politicians, and billionaires.

### How to Use DeepAI
To create AI videos, simply upload your images and text prompts to the AI Video Generator. For generating images, provide a prompt and select an art style in the AI Image Generator. Editing images is quick and easy with the AI Image Editor. And for chatting with AI characters, explore various categories and engage in conversations.

### Pricing
DeepAI offers a PRO membership for $4.99 per month, which includes features like AI Generator calls, AI Video Generator calls, AI Chat messages, Genius Mode messages, Genius Mode images, ad-free experience, private images, and API access. There is also a pay-as-you-go option available for users who prefer a more flexible payment structure.
