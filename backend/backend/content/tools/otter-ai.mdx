### What is Otter.ai?
Otter.ai is an AI meeting note taker and real-time AI transcription tool that helps businesses, sales teams, educators, and media professionals to improve their meetings and create detailed notes effortlessly.

### Features of Otter.ai
- Automatically takes notes during meetings
- Helps in closing deals and onboarding new customers faster
- Assists students and faculty in succeeding in education
- Enables media professionals to create compelling stories using detailed notes

### How Otter.ai Works
Otter.ai uses AI technology to automatically join Zoom, Google Meet, and Microsoft Teams meetings to take notes in real-time. It condenses lengthy meetings into 30-second summaries, captures and assigns action items, and integrates with various tools for seamless workflow automation.
