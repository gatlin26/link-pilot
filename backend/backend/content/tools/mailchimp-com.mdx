### What is Mailchimp?

Mailchimp is a marketing automation and email platform that offers a wide range of services to help businesses connect with their audience through personalized emails, websites, social media marketing, templates, SMS marketing, marketing automation, reporting & analytics, audience management, AI marketing tools, and content creation tools.

### Features of Mailchimp

- ### Email Marketing: Send personalized emails that convert.
- ### Websites: Create your branded web presence.
- ### Social Media Marketing: Amplify the conversation across multiple channels.
- ### Templates: Customize pre-designed layouts.
- ### SMS Marketing: Reach your customers on every device.
- ### Marketing Automation: Deliver the right message at the right time.
- ### Reporting & Analytics: Track sales & campaign performance.
- ### Audience Management: Target and segment customers.
- ### AI Marketing Tools: Say hello to your AI growth assistant.
- ### Content Creation Tools: Build content that captivates and converts.
- ### Integrations: Connect with over 300 apps like Shopify, Wix, Salesforce, Canva, and WooCommerce.

### How Mailchimp Works

Mailchimp helps businesses drive results through seamlessly integrated SMS campaigns, generative AI tools for content creation, advanced audience segmentation, analytics & reporting for performance tracking, and personalized emails based on customer behavior.

### Pricing

Mailchimp offers different pricing plans starting with the Standard plan that allows users to send up to 6,000 emails each month. The pricing varies based on the number of contacts and features included in each plan.
