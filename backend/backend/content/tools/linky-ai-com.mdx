### What is Linky AI?

- ### Chat with Various Characters AI: Connect with a diverse range of AI personas, each with distinct traits and personalities.
- ### Immersive AI Chat Roleplay: Engage in deep, meaningful roleplays with AI characters taking on specific roles.
