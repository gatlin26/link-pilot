### What is Prolific?
Prolific is a platform that allows you to quickly find research participants you can trust. It provides access to a diverse and high-quality participant pool for various research needs.

### Features
- ### Free Representative Samples: Access to representative samples for your research.
- ### Domain Expert Participants: Engage with participants who are experts in specific domains.
- ### Bring Your Own Participants: Ability to bring your own participants to the platform.
- ### Prolific API: Integration capabilities with the Prolific API for seamless operations.

### How it Works
Prolific ensures data quality by vetting and verifying its 200,000+ active participants. The platform conducts ongoing checks to maintain the integrity of the participant pool and offers rich, accurate, and comprehensive responses for research projects.

### Price
Prolific offers transparent and response-based costs for utilizing its participant pool and services. Users can explore costs before committing to any payments.
