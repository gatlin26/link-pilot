### What is Runway?
Runway is a platform that advances creativity through artificial intelligence. It offers AI tools and studios to empower filmmakers and creatives to bring their visions to life.

### Features of Runway
- Runway offers AI tools such as Text to Video, Video to Video, Text to Image, Image to Image, Frame Interpolation, Generative Audio, and Upscale Image.
- Runway Studios empower filmmakers with AI to achieve their creative vision.
- Initiatives like AI Film Festival, Creative Partners Program, and GEN:48 support and celebrate artists using AI in filmmaking.
