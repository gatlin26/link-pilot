### What is Klaviyo?
Klaviyo is a comprehensive marketing automation platform that offers services for email marketing, SMS marketing, and customer data platform (CDP) integration. It provides tools for creating personalized customer experiences across various channels.

### Features of Klaviyo
- ### Email Marketing: Send rich, engaging emails with dynamic content and recommended products.
- ### SMS Marketing: Reach your most engaged VIP customers and engage in two-way conversations.
- ### Ecommerce Platform Integrations: Easily connect your entire tech stack with 350+ pre-built integrations.
- ### Automation Platform: Build automated flows and campaigns to streamline your marketing efforts.
- ### Campaign Builder: Create and customize marketing campaigns tailored to your audience.
- ### Segmentation: Divide your audience into targeted segments for more personalized interactions.
- ### Customer Growth: Utilize AI-driven insights to guide your marketing strategies and boost customer growth.

### How Klaviyo Works
Klaviyo works by consolidating your customer data and providing AI-driven insights to enhance your marketing efforts. By unifying, transforming, and activating your data, Klaviyo helps you understand your customers better, recommend products they'll love, and track success across various channels.
