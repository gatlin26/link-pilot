## Introduction

At BuildWay, we strive to ensure complete customer satisfaction with our services. This Refund Policy outlines the terms and conditions for refunds on our subscription plans.

## Subscription Plans

### Cancellation

You may cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings or by contacting our support team. Upon cancellation:

- You will retain access to your plan features until the end of your current billing period
- No prorated refunds will be issued for partial billing periods
- Your account will automatically downgrade to the Free plan after the billing period ends

### Refund Eligibility

- **Within 7 days of initial purchase**: If you are not satisfied with our service, you may request a full refund within 7 days of your first subscription payment
- **After 7 days**: Refunds are generally not available, but we may consider exceptions on a case-by-case basis

## How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact us at:

- **Email**: support@buildway.cc

Please include the following information in your request:

1. Your account email address
2. Order or transaction ID
3. Reason for the refund request

## Response Time

We respond to all refund requests within **3 business days**. Our team will review your request and notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund.

## Refund Processing

Approved refunds will be processed using the original payment method:

- Credit card refunds may take 5-10 business days to appear on your statement
- The exact timing depends on your payment provider

## Exceptions

Refunds may be denied in the following cases:

- Evidence of abuse or fraudulent activity
- Violation of our Terms of Service
- Excessive refund requests from the same account

## Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Refund Policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting to this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
